What is a Slow Roll

A Slow Roll is a free, community-focused bicycle ride designed for all ages and abilities. Originating in Detroit, Michigan, these gatherings prioritize inclusivity and social connection over speed or athleticism. Typically, Slow Rolls follow a leisurely pace, often around 10 miles per hour, allowing participants to enjoy the scenery, chat with fellow riders, and explore different parts of their city. They often feature unique routes, highlighting diverse neighborhoods and fostering a sense of community pride. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or just dusting off your old bike, Slow Rolls offer a welcoming atmosphere to experience the joy of cycling alongside others.
